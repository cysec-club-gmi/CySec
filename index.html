WHO WE ARE

GMi CySec Club is a passionate and active cybersecurity community at the German-Malaysian Institute, founded in 2024. What began as a group of friends has grown into a thriving club united by a shared interest in cybersecurity, ethical hacking, and digital defense. We actively engage in CTF competitions, awareness-sharing sessions, and hands-on learning experiences to nurture skilled, ethical, and future-ready cybersecurity talents.

MISSION & VISION

Our vision is to be a leading student-driven cybersecurity community that empowers future professionals and bridges the gap between students and the industry. To achieve this, our mission is to develop cybersecurity skills among students through active participation in CTF competitions, workshops, and real-world projects; promote awareness of digital security and responsible online behavior within and beyond the campus; foster a supportive environment that encourages continuous learning, leadership, and professional growth; and connect academic learning with real-world application through community-driven initiatives and industry collaboration.

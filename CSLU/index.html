WHAT IS CSLU?

A technical workshop led by a renowned cybersecurity practitioner, tailored for Malaysian university students eager to advance their practical skills. It bridges academic learning with real-world application, focusing on industry-relevant tools and techniques to prepare participants for careers in cybersecurity.

OUR PARTNER

RE:HACK is a boutique Malaysian cybersecurity company that focuses on research and delivering high-quality services. Their mission is to provide top-tier cybersecurity solutions to both the community and industry. One of their core pillars, RE:EDUCATE, aims to support professional growth within the community. To this end, they actively invest their time, effort, and resources into developing and mentoring students and beginners. Additionally, RE:HACK emphasizes hands-on, practical training to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry needs. Through initiatives such as Cyber Skills Level Up, RE:UN10N and the Malaysia Cybersecurity Camp, RE:HACK equips students and aspiring professionals with the skills and real-world experience needed to thrive in todayâ€™s dynamic cybersecurity landscape.

ABOUT CYSEC

A passionate and active student-driven cybersecurity community at the German-Malaysian Institute, founded in 2024. What began as a group of friends has grown into a thriving club united by a shared interest in cybersecurity, ethical hacking, and digital defense. We actively engage in CTF competitions, awareness-sharing sessions, and hands-on learning experiences to nurture skilled, ethical, and future-ready cybersecurity talents.
